## Summary

Provide repository hardening and drift controls to prevent accidental plaintext commits and CI drift regressions.

## Acceptance Criteria

- `harden` updates `.gitignore` to ignore plaintext secrets while ensuring vault artifacts stay tracked.
- `harden` can install or emit pre-commit and pre-push checks for plaintext/drift guardrails.
- `status` surfaces dirty, missing, vault-only, and conflict/divergence states.
- `lock --check` and `status --fail-if-dirty` provide non-zero CI gating behavior.

## Verification

- Validate hook behavior and drift checks in local workflows and CI simulations.
- Confirm `.gitignore` mutations do not hide committed vault artifacts.

## Notes

Guardrails target accidental exposure and inconsistent repository state.
