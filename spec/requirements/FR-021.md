## Summary

`rm` must remove vault entries safely with pattern support and atomic updates.

## Acceptance Criteria

- `rm` removes selected entries from vault and manifest with pattern support.
- `--remove-local` controls optional plaintext deletion.
- Operation is atomic.

## Verification

- Validate removal semantics, pattern matching, and atomic behavior.
