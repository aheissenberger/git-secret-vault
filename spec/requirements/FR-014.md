## Summary

Deliver maintenance and diagnostics commands for integrity checks, cleanup safety, environment diagnostics, and hardening/compatibility flows.

## Acceptance Criteria

- `verify` decrypts/validates manifest, checks entry hashes, detects corruption, and supports `--json`.
- `clean` removes unlocked tracked plaintext with confirmation and `--force`, never deleting untracked files.
- `doctor` reports keyring, permission, vault/index validity, compatibility expectations, and remediation guidance.
- `compat check` and `harden` expose behavior defined in their dedicated requirements.

## Verification

- Validate corruption detection scenarios and verify JSON outputs.
- Validate clean safety constraints and doctor diagnostics coverage.

## Notes

These commands provide operational safety and supportability controls.
