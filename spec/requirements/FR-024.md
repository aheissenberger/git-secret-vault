## Summary

`verify` must validate manifest decryption, entry hashes, and corruption scenarios with JSON output support.

## Acceptance Criteria

- `verify` validates manifest decryptability and entry integrity hashes.
- Corruption/mismatch conditions are detected and reported.
- `--json` output is supported.

## Verification

- Validate successful and corrupted vault verification paths.

Derived from the SafeZipVault source requirements for verify behavior.
